My Summer Adventures

My Trip To California

It was to long,quick trip.We loaded our vehicle and headed to Northen California for
the 4th of July weekend for a family ruion.We then made haste to Magic Mountain.
By the time we returned home,we had traveled some 1,300miles in three and one
half days.

My Trip To The California

After a year's preparation,it was time to go backpacking with the Boys Scouts.We flew
to Colorado Springs,toured the Air Force Academy,Pike's Peak,Garden of The Gods,
and the Olympic training center.we chartered a bus to the Philmont Scout Ranch in
Northen New Mexico and began our 60 mile backpack trek.Afterwards,we boarded
Amtrack's and rode the rails to Kingman, and then chartered the bus home.

My Trip To The Mid West

My wife and I drove our son's red Ford Mustang some 2,900 miles to him in
Indiana where he attends college.In route,we stopped at Nauvoo,Illinois,and
Detroit,Michigan.We then boarded Amtrak in Chicago,and rode it to Salt Lake city,
where we boarded a bus which returned us to Las Vegas.
